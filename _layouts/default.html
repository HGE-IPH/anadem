ANADEM - South America Digital Terrain Model

ANADEM is a digital terrain model (DTM) for the South America. The model removes the vegetation bias from the Copernicus GLO-30 DEM by appling a new methodology based on remote sensing data and machine learning.

Data is freely available at 30 meters in Google Earth Engine platform or in tif files located in the Download section in this page.

Earth Engine Snippet

var anadem = ee.ImageCollection("projects/et-brasil/assets/anadem/v1");
